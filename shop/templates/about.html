About Us



Bright As A Button is a homemade hand-knitted hat business.

We started out in 2017 and have become known around the local craft markets. We make warm wooly for both children and adults in a wide range of styles and colours.

Hats can be bought from the online shop or we can custom make hats to your preference for a one of kind accessory.
Simply contact us through any platform or email! :)
